Macinball PlusVR

Winner | Uncommon Hacks 2016

This was my introduction to VR and developing for it. Macinball Plus VR is a Multiplayer VR game using Leap Motion that was created and won in Uncommon Hacks Hackathon at University of Chicago.

Not only was this my introduction to VR, but also hand tracking, device networking, and designing a virtual environment in Unity.
