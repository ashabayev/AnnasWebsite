Macinball PlusVR

Winner | Uncommon Hacks 2016

This was my introduction to VR and developing for it. Macinball Plus VR is a Multiplayer VR game using Leap Motion that was created and won in Uncommon Hacks Hackathon at University of Chicago. In the game, players can interact with eachother and be able to pick up the balls in the virtual environment by using their real hands. When the balls hit an object, they make a sound related to their image, and the sound was represented accurately in space.

Not only was this my introduction to VR, but also hand tracking, device networking, and designing a virtual environment in Unity.
