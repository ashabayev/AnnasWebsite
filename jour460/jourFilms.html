Life Out of the House: Covid19 | May 2020

Chicago O'Hare International Airport



Boston Commons

While setting up for the first shot, a couple of interesting things happened that weren't caught on camera. First, I saw some protestors walking away in full American flag attire. Next, saw a man on a longboard playing movie dialogue through a loudspeaker while wearing a gas mask. And then saw a Trump supporter recording people and intentionally going in their personal space and breaking social distancing rules.
