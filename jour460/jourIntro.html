Intro

Life Out of the House: Covid19 | May 2020

Covid19

Welcome to May 2020 during the Covid19 pandemic. There is a stay at home order for all non essential workers to quarentine themselves as the United States attempts to flatten the curve of Covid19 cases. It is a drastic change from normalcy, and I wanted to know how are people reacting to it. How has public spaces changed to accomodate for this? How do people interact with eachother when they are out of the house? I seeked to investigate how public life has been impacted during this pandemic, while keeping myself and others around me safe as I took a 360 camera around the Chicago airport and around Boston commons.
