USC Creating Reality 2018

Social Microsoft Hololens experience created in the Creating Reality Hackathon at University of Southern California to teach proper breathing techniques.

One of the biggest challenges with this project was the miscommunication between the programmers and the artists. When I realized this, I decided to step up and bridge the gap between the two. For me, this was easy because I familiar with being a programmer and with being a designer. I understood the thought processes, vocabulary, and motivations on both sides, which led to a successful final product.

My role was to program a way to connect the hololens and phones together and to help design the experience. Afterwards, I modeled the assets.
